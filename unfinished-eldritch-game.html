UNFINISHED ELDRITCH GAME

You are the monster. Destroy everything.

Game Jam Arcade Shoot-em-up Monster
// project_overview

Unfinished Eldritch Game is an arcade-style shoot-em-up where you play as a giant eldritch monster on a rampage. The city is your playground — smash skyscrapers, swat attack helicopters out of the sky, and reduce civilization to rubble.

Think Rampage meets cosmic horror. You control the "Squidboi" — a tentacled abomination with a chip on its shoulder and a city to demolish. No puzzles, no diplomacy. Just destruction.
