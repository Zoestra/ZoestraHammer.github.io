VAPORBOI

Deliver. Throw. Repeat.

Game Jam VR Sports Simulation
// project_overview

VaporBoi is a VR newspaper delivery game that plays like frisbee golf. Grab a stack of papers, arc your throws, and nail every porch on the block — all while cruising through a neon-soaked synthwave neighborhood straight out of the 80s.

Each level is a new route through the city. Wind, distance, and obstacles keep every toss interesting. Hit the doorstep for bonus points. Miss and watch the paper flutter into a bush. Simple mechanics, endless vibe.
