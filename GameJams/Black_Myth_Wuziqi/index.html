BLACK MYTH: WUZIQI

Gomoku. Powerups. Chaos.

Game Jam Strategy Powerups 2D
// project_overview

Black Myth: Wuziqi takes the classic Chinese board game of Gomoku (五子棋) and cranks it up with powerup mechanics. It's still five in a row — but now every few turns you can drop a special stone that clears a line, blocks your opponent, or reshuffles the board.

Wrapped in a dark fantasy aesthetic inspired by Black Myth: Wukong, each match feels like a duel on a sacred mountain. The rules are simple. The powerups are not.
